About

Haze Unified Alerting Platform is an aggregator for weather information and emergency alerts.

It brings public warning feeds, weather products, audio playout, public listening, and telephone access into one deployable system for communities, hobbyists, broadcasters, and emergency communications operators.

The project is designed around an event-driven architecture so independent services can ingest data, render products, synthesize speech, broadcast audio, and expose public access without one component owning the entire workflow.
